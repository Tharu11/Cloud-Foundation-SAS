14_SAS_On What is IAAS.




1. What does it take for an organization to build an Infrastructure?
ANS. To build an Infrastructure for an organization they need servers, hardwares, routers, switches, firewalls, cabling, a lot of security, monitoring, log access, load balancing, clustering, storage and much more. 
	

2. What is infrastructure as a service provider?
ANS. Infrastructure as a service provider means infrastructure as a service provider will provide server, hardwares, routers, switches, firewalls, cabling, a lot of security, monitoring, log access, load balancing, clustering, storage which makes an infrastructure as a service. 
	

3. Infrastructure as a service provider provides all the necessary infrastructure so users don’t have to build their own infrastructure? True/False.
ANS. True.
	

4. Where will all the infrastructure be and managed by?
ANS. All the infrastructure will be with the infrastructure as a service provider and they will be the one’s managing the infrastructure.
	

5. List the cloud service providers who will provide the infrastructure as a service?
ANS. The cloud service providers such as MS Azure, AWS, IBM Cloud will provide the infrastructure as a service.
	

6. As an infrastructure as a service customer all you have to do is have access to the internet and a subscription with cloud service provider? True/False.
ANS. True.
	

7. Why would a business choose infrastructure as a service?
ANS. A business would choose infrastructure as a service because it is very easy, fast and cost efficient to operate a workload.
	

8. Is IAAS effective for the workload that is temporary? True/False.
ANS. True.
	

9. What can a user rent from infrastructure as a service?
ANS. From infrastructure as a service a user can rent IT infrastructure servers, virtual machines, storage, networks, and operating systems from cloud providers.
	

10. With infrastructure as a service there are no upfront costs? True/False.
ANS. True.
